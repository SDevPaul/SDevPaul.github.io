I've crafted a non-linear game that puts a twisted spin on the classic Red Riding Hood tale. Rather than merely reading, players actively embody Red Riding Hood, guiding her through a chilling narrative.

Drawing inspiration from dating sims and interactive narratives, the game's design revolves around dialogue choices with NPCs. A UI box at the bottom displays dialogue options, while characters' reactions are shown above, creating dynamic interactions that shape the storyline.

My aim was to merge traditional storytelling with engaging gameplay mechanics, injecting elements of horror for a fresh take on the fairy tale. Meticulously designed visuals and interface elements heighten immersion, ensuring players are captivated by the unfolding narrative.

This seamless blend of narrative and gameplay offers a unique experience with each playthrough, with choices dictating varied outcomes. I'm confident this innovative approach will deliver a compelling and memorable gaming experience, bridging classic tales with modern interactive storytelling.
