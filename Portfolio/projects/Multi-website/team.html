Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Creator: Kanehito Yamada (writer) • Tsukasa Abe (illustrator)

Creator

Kanehito Yamada (writer)
( 山田鐘人; Yamada Kanehito) is a japanese mangaka. They are The Author Of Nanashi Wa Ittai Dare Deshou? And Frieren: Beyond Journey's End. Also Known For Being The Author & Artist Of Bocchi Hakase To Robot Shoujo No Zetsubou Teki Utopia.

Tsukasa Abe (illustrator)
more commonly written as Abe Tsukasa 「アベツカサ, Abe Tsukasa?」, is the illustrator for the Frieren: Beyond Journey's End manga series.
