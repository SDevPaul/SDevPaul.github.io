The concept of our project was to create a dynamic video showcasing the intricate movements of a car, including drifting, speeding, and changing batteries. Using stop-motion techniques, we brought these movements to life with a mouse as our subject. Drawing from our previous experiences and lessons in videography, we aimed to produce an engaging and entertaining piece. Throughout the production process, we brainstormed numerous ideas, but time constraints prevented us from realizing all of them.

Despite this, we are proud of the final outcome. We meticulously used sound effects to replicate the sounds of a car and selected music to create an intense atmosphere that keeps viewers engaged. The video comprises over 3,000 pictures and took approximately three days to complete. I am proud of the result and deeply appreciative of my groupmates, whose collaboration made this project enjoyable and successful.
