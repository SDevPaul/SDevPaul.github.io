Academic Project

Our videography project showcases a compelling story of students attending the Metaverseage Training Institute, providing an immersive tour of its facilities, lobbies, classrooms, and more. We utilized the Nikon D5500 and Samsung S21 FE to capture both photography and videography, ensuring high-quality visuals from multiple perspectives. For the editing process, we employed Microsoft Clipchamp, seamlessly integrating sound effects and music sourced from YouTube to enhance the narrative. This collaborative effort involved the dedicated contributions of Julianne Febrero, Muskan Asif, Maryam, and I, resulting in a professional and engaging visual presentation.
